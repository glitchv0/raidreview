### Priestsky (Discipline Priest) - CRITICAL

**Average HPS:** 61k
**Average Parse:** 18%

| Fight | HPS | Parse | Notes |
|-------|-----|-------|-------|
| Megaera | 82k | 45% | Best performance |
| Jin'rokh (10H) | 61k | 4% | Critical |
| Tortos | 59k | 17% | Poor |
| Council | 52k | 3% | Critical |
| Horridon | 50k | 21% | Poor |

**Analysis:**
- 4% on Jin'rokh and 3% on Council are extremely concerning
- Only Megaera showed reasonable performance (45%)
- HPS consistently lower than co-healer

**Comparison to Kamakmeleon:**

| Fight | Priestsky HPS | Kamakmeleon HPS | Gap |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|-----|
| Jin'rokh (10H) | 61k | 100k | -39% |
| Horridon | 50k | 48k | +4% |
| Council | 52k | 78k | -33% |
| Tortos | 59k | 82k | -28% |
| Megaera | 82k | 121k | -32% |

Priestsky is doing 25-40% less healing than the co-healer on most fights.

**Likely Issues:**

1. **Atonement Healing**
   - Penance may not be used on cooldown
   - Smite filler insufficient
   - Holy Fire on cooldown?

2. **Spirit Shell**
   - Timing appears off or not used
   - Should be used BEFORE major damage

3. **Prayer of Mending**
   - Likely underused
   - Should be cast on cooldown (10s CD)

4. **Power Word: Shield**
   - May not be blanketing efficiently
   - Rapture timing for mana

**Recommendations:**

1. **CRITICAL: Atonement Rotation**
   - Penance on cooldown (enemy target)
   - Smite as filler
   - Holy Fire on cooldown
   - Atonement should be 30-40% of healing

2. **Spirit Shell**
   - Use 15s BEFORE major damage
   - Converts healing into absorbs
   - Critical for: Ionization, Static Burst, etc.

3. **Prayer of Mending**
   - Cast on cooldown
   - 4-5 min fight = 25-30+ casts expected

4. **Archangel Usage**
   - Build 5 Evangelism stacks
   - Pop Archangel for 25% healing buff
   - Should have 8+ uses per fight

5. **Cooldowns**
   - Power Infusion: On cooldown
   - Pain Suppression: Tank cooldown
   - Barrier: Stack for mechanics
